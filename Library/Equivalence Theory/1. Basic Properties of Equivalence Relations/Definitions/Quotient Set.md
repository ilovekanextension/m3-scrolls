**Definition.** Let $A$ be a set. Let $\sim$ be an equivalence relation on $A$. The **quotient** of $A$ by $\sim$ is the set $$A/{\sim}=\{[a]_{\sim}\subseteq A\mid a\in A\}$$of all equivalence classes of members of $A$.
