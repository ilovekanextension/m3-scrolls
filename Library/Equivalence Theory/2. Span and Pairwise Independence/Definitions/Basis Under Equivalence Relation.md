**Definition.** Let $A$ be a set. Let $\sim$ be an equivalence relation on $A$. Suppose $B\subseteq A$. We say that $B$ is a **basis** for $A$ (**under** $\sim$) if $B$ spans $A$ and is pairwise independent under $\sim$.
