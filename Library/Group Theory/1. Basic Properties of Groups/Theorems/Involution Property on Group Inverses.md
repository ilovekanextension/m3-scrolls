**Theorem.** Let $G$ be a group. Then, for all $a\in G$, $(a^{-1})^{-1}=a$.

**Proof.** Suppose $a\in G$. We have $$(a^{-1})^{-1}\circ a^{-1}=1=a\circ a^{-1},$$so by [[Cancellation Properties on Groups]] $(a^{-1})^{-1}=a$. $\blacksquare$
