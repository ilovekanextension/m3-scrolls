**Theorem.** Let $M$ be a monoid.
1. $\{1\}$ is a submonoid of $M$.
2. $M$ is a submonoid of $M$.

**Proof of First Statement.** By definition, $1\in\{1\}$. Also, $1\circ 1=1$. $\blacksquare$

**Proof of Second Statement.** This is clear. $\blacksquare$
