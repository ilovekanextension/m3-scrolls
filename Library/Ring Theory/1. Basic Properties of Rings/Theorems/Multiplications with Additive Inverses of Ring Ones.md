**Theorem.** Let $R$ be a ring. Then, for all $a\in R$, $-1\cdot a=-a$ and $a\cdot (-1)=-a$.

**Proof.** Suppose $a\in R$. By [[Multiplications with Additive Inverses]], $$-1\cdot a=-(1\cdot a)=-a$$and $$a\cdot(-1)=-(a\cdot 1)=-a.\blacksquare$$
