**Theorem.** Let $R$ be a ring. Suppose that $0=1$. Then, for all $a\in R$, $a=0$. Therefore, the set $R$ is the singleton set $\{0\}$.

**Proof.** Suppose $a\in R$. By [[Multiplications with Ring Zeros]],
$$\begin{align}
a & =a\circ 1 \\
 & =a\circ 0 \\
 & =0.\blacksquare
\end{align}$$
