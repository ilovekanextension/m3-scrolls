**Theorem.** Let $R$ be a commutative ring. Then, for all $a\in R$, $a\mid 0$.

**Proof.** By [[Multiplications with Ring Zeros]], $$0=0\cdot a.$$Therefore, $a\mid 0$. $\blacksquare$
