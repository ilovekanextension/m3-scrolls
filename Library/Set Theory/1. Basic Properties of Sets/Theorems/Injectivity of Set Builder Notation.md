**Theorem.** Let $P$ and $Q$ be propositions. Suppose that $$\{x\mid P(x)\}=\{x\mid Q(x)\}.$$Then, for all objects $x$, we have $P(x)$ if and only if $Q(x)$.

**Proof.** We have
$$\begin{align}
P(x) & \iff x\in\{x\mid P(x)\} \\
 & \iff x\in\{x\mid Q(x)\} \\
 & \iff Q(x). \blacksquare
\end{align}$$
