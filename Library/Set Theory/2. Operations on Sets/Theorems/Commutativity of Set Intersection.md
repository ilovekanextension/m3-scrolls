**Theorem.** Let $A$ and $B$ be sets. Then, $$A\cap B=B\cap A.$$
**Proof.** Suppose $x$ is an object. We have
$$\begin{align}
x\in A\cap B & \iff x\in A\wedge x\in B \\
 & \iff x\in B\wedge x\in A \\
 & \iff x\in B\cap A. \blacksquare
\end{align}$$
