**Theorem.** Let $A$ and $B$ be sets. Then, $$A\cup B=B\cup A.$$
**Proof.** Suppose $x$ is an object. We have
$$\begin{align}
x\in A\cup B & \iff x\in A\vee x\in B \\
 & \iff x\in B\vee x\in A \\
 & \iff x\in B\cup A. \blacksquare
\end{align}$$
