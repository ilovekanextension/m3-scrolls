**Theorem.** Let $A$ and $B$ be sets. Then, $$\bigcap\{A,B\}=A\cap B.$$
**Proof.** Suppose $x$ is an object. We have
$$\begin{align}
x\in\bigcap \{A,B\} & \iff \forall X\in\{A,B\} \ (x\in X) \\
 & \iff x\in A\wedge x\in B \\
 & \iff x\in A\cap B. \blacksquare
\end{align}$$
