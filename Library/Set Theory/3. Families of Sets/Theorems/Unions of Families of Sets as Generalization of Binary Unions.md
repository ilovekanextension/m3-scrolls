**Theorem.** Let $A$ and $B$ be sets. Then, $$\bigcup\{A,B\}=A\cup B.$$
**Proof.** Suppose $x$ is an object. We have
$$\begin{align}
x\in\bigcup\{A,B\} & \iff \exists X\in\{A,B\} \ (x\in X) \\
 & \iff x\in A\vee x\in B \\
 & \iff x\in A\cup B. \blacksquare
\end{align}$$
