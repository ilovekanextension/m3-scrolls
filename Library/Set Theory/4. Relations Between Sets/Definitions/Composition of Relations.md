**Definition.** Let $A$, $B$, and $C$ be sets. Let $R\subseteq A\times B$ and $S\subseteq B\times C$. The **composition** of $R$ with $S$ is the relation $$S\circ R=\{(a,c)\in A\times C\mid \exists b\in B \ (aRb\wedge bRc)\}.$$
