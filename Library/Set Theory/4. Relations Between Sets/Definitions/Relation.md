**Definition.** Let $A$ and $B$ be sets. A set $R\subseteq A\times B$ is called a **relation** from $A$ to $B$. If $R\subseteq A\times B$ and $(a,b)\in R$, we may write $$aRb.$$A relation from $A$ to $A$ is called a relation **on** $A$.
