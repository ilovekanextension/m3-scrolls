**Theorem.** Let $A$ and $B$ be sets. Let $R\subseteq A\times B$. Then, $$(R^{-1})^{-1}=R.$$
**Proof.** Suppose $(a,b)\in A\times B$. We have
$$\begin{align}
(a,b)\in(R^{-1})^{-1} & \iff (b,a)\in R^{-1} \\
 & \iff (a,b)\in R.\blacksquare
\end{align}$$
