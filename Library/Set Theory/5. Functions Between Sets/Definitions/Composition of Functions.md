**Definition.** Let $A$, $B$, and $C$ be sets. Let $f:A\to B$ and $g:B\to C$. By [[Compositions of Functions as Functions]], $g\circ f$ is a function from $A$ to $C$. We call this function as the **composition** of $f$ and $g$.
